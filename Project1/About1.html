About Titanic

Titanic is a 1997 epic romance, drama and disaster film starring Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet, and Billy Zane. It was directed by James Cameron.It was a fictionalized account of the sinking of the RMS Titanic and followed the forbidden romance of a beautiful first-class woman (Kate Winslet) and a penniless third-class artist (Leonardo DiCaprio) aboard the ill-fated maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic.
